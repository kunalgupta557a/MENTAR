Frequently Asked Questions

Why "Floor" technology doesn't support my phone?

Because your phone doesn't support "Googles's AR services", You can still use Magic paper, you just need a print of it and change the system to Magic paper in AR button

How to use Magic paper?

A magic paper is like a QR code that gives an augmented experience. Users can access the content by scanning the paper using their mobile camera.

What is MentAR Scholarship?

MentAR Scholarship is a micro programme build to encourage learning in Indian Students by proving an incentive to it in a fun way.
Students can take 10 min curriculum-based quizzes and get INR 50 scholarship per quiz within 24 hours (INR 1,000 for 20 such quizzes).

How to redeem your scholarship?

By processing your KYC with your school ID.
